Factors that increase the price

Integrations with banks or payment gateways, regulatory compliance (GDPR, financial sector), complex roles and permissions, legacy data migration, and high availability (99.9% SLA) increase the budget. Agile methodology with phased deliveries allows spreading the investment.

Custom development vs template vs low-code

Templates (WordPress, Shopify) are cheap but limited. Low-code accelerates prototypes but can increase long-term maintenance costs. Custom software has a higher initial cost but fits exactly with your operations and scales with you.
