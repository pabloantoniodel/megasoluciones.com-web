Choosing between RPA, automation with APIs, or low-code platforms defines the cost, maintenance, and reliability of your automated processes. This comparison helps you decide.

RPA (Robotic Process Automation)

Simulates clicks and keystrokes in applications without an API. Ideal for legacy software or old portals. Slower and fragile to interface changes, but indispensable when there is no alternative.

Automation with APIs

Connects systems directly (Odoo, Salesforce, Google Sheets). Faster, more stable, and cheaper to maintain. Recommended whenever the tool allows it.

Low-code (Zapier, Make, n8n)

Excellent for prototypes and simple processes. Can become expensive with a high volume of operations or complex logic. n8n self-hosted is a very popular intermediate option in Spain.

Quick comparison table

  • Execution speed: APIs > low-code > RPA
  • Maintenance cost: APIs < low-code < RPA
  • Legacy compatibility: RPA > low-code > APIs
